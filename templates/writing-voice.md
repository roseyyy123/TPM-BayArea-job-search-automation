# Writing voice (template)

Rules for anything written in your voice (cover letters, outreach notes, follow-ups, the planner).

- Tone: e.g., informal, direct, human.
- Banned: AI filler words and phrases ("delve", "leverage", "I hope this finds you well"), em dashes, corporate boilerplate.
- Sentence style: short, concrete, specific.
- Add 2-3 real samples of your writing so the voice can be matched.
